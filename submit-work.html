What happens next?

Your material will be reviewed for suitability, clarity, educational value and permission status before being featured.

  1. Submit your details and resource.
  2. SpeakOut team reviews it.
  3. Approved resources are added to the library.
  4. Your name/title may be credited.
  5. Your work reaches students and low-income learners.
Recognition pathways:
Featured Author, Academic Contributor, Clinical Knowledge Partner, Community Education Contributor.
Important:
Do not submit materials you do not own or have permission to share. Approval is required before public listing.
Automation note:
Automate submission collection, not public publishing. Admin approval must remain mandatory.
