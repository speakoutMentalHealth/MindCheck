🧒 SpeakOut Kiddies Corner

A safe learning environment designed for primary and secondary school students. Build confidence, leadership skills, emotional intelligence, digital literacy, career awareness and personal development through fun and engaging resources.

📚 Primary School Learning

Character Building

Learn honesty, kindness, respect, empathy and responsibility.

Reading Skills

Interactive reading materials and story-based learning.

Personal Safety

Understanding safety at home, school and online.

Confidence Building

Helping children express themselves and believe in their abilities.

🎓 Secondary School Academy

Leadership Development

Learn teamwork, communication and leadership skills.

Public Speaking

Build confidence speaking in class and public settings.

Entrepreneurship

Introduction to business, innovation and problem solving.

Study Skills

Learn how to study smarter and prepare for exams effectively.

💻 Digital Skills

Computer Basics

Introduction to computers and digital literacy.

Microsoft Office

Word, Excel and PowerPoint essentials.

Canva Design

Create graphics, presentations and school projects.

Introduction to AI

Learn how Artificial Intelligence works in everyday life.

🧠 Mental Health & Wellbeing

Understanding Emotions

Recognize and manage feelings in healthy ways.

Stress Management

Simple techniques to manage school pressure.

Bullying Prevention

Learn how to identify and respond to bullying.

Healthy Relationships

Build positive friendships and social skills.
