MindCheck — Volunteer Zone

Get Trained. Make a Difference.

We offer certified volunteer training to equip you with practical skills for safe listening, red-flag triage, and referral pathways in Nigerian communities.

What you’ll gain

  • Confidence to receive and support someone in distress — safely and respectfully.
  • Clear referral steps for emergencies and ongoing care (Abuja, Keffi, Owerri & beyond).
  • Simple, privacy-first recording for impact reporting (schools, partners, grants).
  • Digital certificate and a “Volunteer Trained” badge inside MindCheck.

How it works

  • Access the training portal below (mobile-friendly lessons & short checks).
  • Finish the modules, then complete the quick quiz.
  • Return to the app via index.html?quiz=done to unlock your badge.
