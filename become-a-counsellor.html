Become a Counsellor

Join our verified counsellor network. Take the standard exam, upload your certificate for verification, then go live so users can find and message you for counselling.

Step-by-step

  1. Open the counsellor exam and complete it.
  2. Receive your certificate.
  3. Upload your certificate here for verification.
  4. Once verified, your counsellor profile is created and you appear “Live” in Speak to a Counsellor.
