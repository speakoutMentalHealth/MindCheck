Privacy Notice

Last updated: October 2025

SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the MindCheck Nigeria self-assessment platform. This notice explains how we handle information under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA).

1. What We Collect

  • Anonymous survey responses about mood and anxiety (WHO-5, PHQ-9, GAD-7).
  • Optional contact information (name, phone, or email) only if you choose to share it for follow-up support.
  • Technical data such as time of submission (automatically generated).

2. How We Use the Data

  • To generate your self-check results and identify potential support needs.
  • To improve the quality and reach of mental health awareness programs.
  • To contact you only if you request assistance.

3. Legal Basis

Processing is based on your consent and legitimate interest in promoting mental health awareness.

4. Data Retention

Survey responses are stored securely in encrypted Google systems (Tally + Google Sheets). Contact data is deleted within 6 months of submission unless extended support is requested.

5. Data Security

All data transmission uses HTTPS. Access is restricted to authorized SpeakOut staff trained in data protection.

6. Your Rights

Under NDPA, you may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us below.
