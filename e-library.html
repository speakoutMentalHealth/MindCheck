Copyright-safe E-Library model

SpeakOut should not host copyrighted books without permission. This page links readers to legal public sources such as Open Library, Internet Archive, Project Gutenberg, OpenStax, WHO, UNICEF and OpenWHO. Where PDF/download is legally available, users access it directly from the source site.
