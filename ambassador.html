Developing Mental Health Leaders Across Nigeria

SpeakOut Ambassadors serve as credible representatives of SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach. They lead awareness, reduce stigma, support outreach campaigns and help people access appropriate mental health resources.

Who Can Apply?

The program is open to individuals who are passionate about mental health advocacy, service and responsible leadership.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must demonstrate interest in mental health advocacy.
  • Must show leadership potential and integrity.
  • Must be willing to commit time to ambassador activities.
  • Must be able to represent SpeakOut professionally.
  • Open to students, young professionals, youth volunteers and community leaders.

Selection Process

Ambassador status is not automatic. Every applicant must complete the official application process and receive approval from SpeakOut.

Online Application

Interested candidates must complete the official SpeakOut Ambassador Google Form. Applications are reviewed based on motivation, leadership potential, communication skills and commitment to service.

Screening & Review

The SpeakOut team reviews applications and shortlists candidates who meet the program requirements. Not all applicants will proceed to interview.

Interview Booking

Shortlisted applicants will be contacted and booked for a virtual or physical interview with the NGO team.

Approval & Certification

Only successful applicants who pass the interview and complete onboarding will be approved as SpeakOut Ambassadors and allowed to print their certificate.

Ambassador Responsibilities

SpeakOut Ambassadors are expected to uphold the values, policies and standards of SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach.

Representation

Represent SpeakOut professionally at events, schools, campuses, community programs and online platforms.

Awareness

Promote positive mental health education and reduce stigma through approved campaigns and resources.

Outreach Support

Support SpeakOut campaigns, outreach events, school programs and community initiatives.

Reporting

Submit periodic activity reports, campaign updates and impact records when required.

Confidentiality

Respect privacy and follow safeguarding rules when interacting with vulnerable individuals or communities.

Referral

Guide people to appropriate support channels without diagnosing, treating or making unsafe promises.

Program Benefits

The program gives ambassadors practical experience, leadership exposure and recognition for meaningful service.

  • Official SpeakOut Ambassador Certificate after approval.
  • Digital Ambassador ID after successful onboarding.
  • Access to the Ambassador Community Network.
  • Leadership and project management experience.
  • Mentorship, volunteering and future leadership opportunities.
  • Recognition for outstanding service and impact.

Ambassador Code of Conduct

All ambassadors must demonstrate integrity, professionalism, respect, confidentiality and responsible conduct online and offline.

  • Respect diversity and inclusion.
  • Avoid discrimination, harassment or harmful communication.
  • Follow SpeakOut safeguarding policies.
  • Do not present yourself as a therapist or medical professional unless officially licensed.
  • Represent SpeakOut responsibly at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the program free?

Yes. There is no application fee.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, but certificates are issued only to applicants who pass the recruitment, interview and onboarding process.

Is this a paid position?

The Ambassador Program is primarily voluntary. Ambassadors may receive recognition, awards, stipends or sponsored opportunities for specific approved projects where applicable.

Can I apply from any state?

Yes. Applications are open to eligible individuals across Nigeria and beyond.
